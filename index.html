The Story

The Mission Intensifies

Hamza Ali Mazari — whose real identity is Jaskirat Singh Rangi — has survived the deadliest corridors of Karachi's underworld. Now the undercover Indian intelligence agent must go deeper, face old enemies anew, and settle a blood debt dating back to the 26/11 Mumbai attacks.


The battle between power and resistance intensifies. Past conflicts spill into new confrontations. Loyalty, betrayal, and survival drive every move. Control becomes harder to hold as pressure from all sides reaches a breaking point.


In the sequel, Arjun Rampal's Ilyas Kashmiri steps into centre stage as the principal antagonist, while the backstory of Jaskirat Singh Rangi is finally revealed. Directed and written by Aditya Dhar, this 7-hour duology delivers a complete, uncompromising spy saga.
